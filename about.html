About Let's Mind Our Minds

About lets mind our minds:

My name is Aoibhe Conway and I am the face behind let's mind our minds. I am 19 years old and I live in Swords, County Dublin. I am currently in my second year in university and I am studying Creative Digital Media. I have had a passion for mental health since I was in secondary school, I have been a member of many different organizations and committees and have always enjoyed trying to make a difference. I set up my page to promote mental health and positivity. I believe there is a lot of negativity online and I wanted to change that. I believe that mental health is a topic that isn't spoken about enough and one that is very important. My aim for my page is to help others with their mental health, to spread positivity, and to let people know that it's okay to ask for help. Personally, I have been through a lot in association with mental health and suicide, I have suffered with my own mental health from time to time and I still do but I know how to control it and that is why I share quotes and positive messages that help me in the hopes that others can learn how to mind their mind. My aim is to even help one person with their mental health.
