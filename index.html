What is lets mind our minds?

Welcome to the let’s mind our minds website, thank you for visiting our site today. Here at let’s mind or minds we aim to mind our minds together, mental health is a topic that is very important to speak about but one that is not spoken about enough. My name is Aoibhe Conway and I set up let’s mind our minds nearly three years ago. I hope you enjoy taking a trip around the let’s mind our minds website and I hope you take away a thing or two that could help you mind your mind. Everyone suffers with their own mental health from time to time but it's all about knowing how to control it the best way that you can. To anyone who may be struggling along with their mental health just remember that no matter how alone you feel there is always someone to turn to whether that may be a family member, a friend, a colleague or a helpline there is always someone there who was ready and willing to help you. It’s okay not to feel okay and it’s also okay to ask for help.
